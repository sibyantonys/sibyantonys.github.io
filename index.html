About Company

Started in 1965 by P.D. Antony, at Jews street, Cochin, Staines Trading Company was a pioneer in baking products segment. All the major bakeries & hotels in Kerala have been customers of Staines. From a humble beginning, Staines trading company has grown into a business conglomerate having operations all over Kerala with branches in Kollam & Trivandrum. The involvement of the younger generation of the family has resulted in considerable growth and giving a modern face to the organization.Staines trading company has been an inspiration to young entrepreneurs in the food industry. The outlets of Staines have also supported many aspiring women entrepreneurs in full filling their dreams of owning an enterprise. Staines has well trained professional staff to manage business operations. Staines employs about 50 persons across all branches. Staines believes that the secret of their success lies in the satisfaction of its customers & its employees. Staines show rooms display wide range of products catering to the varied demands of the hotel & baking segment & are C&F’s for major food brands in the state.
